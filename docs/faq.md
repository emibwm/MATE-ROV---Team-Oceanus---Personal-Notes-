# FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions for Team Oceanus.

## General
- **What is MATE ROV?**
  [MATE ROV]([url](https://materovcompetition.org/)) is an underwater robotics competition that challenges students to solve real-world problems while gaining career-level experience. Team Oceanus will be competing within the [Explorer Class]([url](https://materovcompetition.org/explorer)), that is, the most advanced level of this competition, which means that we must work hard, apply the systems of engineering properly and try our best if we want to show MATE ROV how great Oceanus is!


## Delivery Documents 
- **How do you want me to work on the assembly procedure?**
  Think of the assembly procedure for our ROV as the instructions provided by LEGO or IKEA. They should be intuitive, highly visual and easy to follow even for someone with no prior experience. It should incude step-by-step instructions with clear diagrams, labeled components and minimal text. Each step specifies the required parts, tools, and orientations, ensuring accuracy during assembly. Aditionally, it should contain safety notes and key specifications such as dimensions and connections to prevent errors and ensure functionality!
