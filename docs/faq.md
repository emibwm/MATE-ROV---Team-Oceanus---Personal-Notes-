## Delivery Documents 
- **How do you want me to work on the assembly procedure?**
  Think of the ROV as a lego set, which requires instructions for it to be built by people that have no clue on how to build it. That is the purpose of the assembly procedure, and I want you to make it just like LEGO or IKEA does it, lots of visuals, clear steps and with all the required specifications!
