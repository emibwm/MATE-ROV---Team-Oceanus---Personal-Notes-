# Contributing to Team Oceanus

This repository serves as a central hub for our work in underwater robotics, engineering systems, and technical documentation. Whether you're new to the team or continuing the journey with us, you are more than welcome to explore, learn, and contribute as we take a deep dive into innovation, this year and beyond °❀⋆.ೃ࿔*:･ 

## Team Guidelines

1.  **Be Collaborative:** Share your ideas and help others. We are a team after all! 
2.  **Maintain Documentation:** Ensure any new features or changes are documented in the `docs/` folder.
3.  **Use Issue Templates:** When reporting bugs or suggesting features, use the templates provided in `.github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/`.
4.  **Code Quality:** Follow the established coding standards for our ROV software.
5.  **Safety First:** Always prioritize safety in both hardware and software development, as outlined in `docs/safety.md`.

## How to Contribute

*   **Fork the repo:** Create your own branch for changes.
*   **Submit Pull Requests:** Explain your changes clearly in the PR description.
*   **Participate in Reviews:** Be open to feedback and participate in reviewing others' work.

Thank you for helping Team Oceanus succeed!
